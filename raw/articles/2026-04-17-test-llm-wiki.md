## Core Ideas

LLM Wiki is a persistent knowledge base concept proposed by Andrej Karpathy,
which differs from traditional RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) by being
**stateful** and **compounding** over time.

Key operations:
- Ingest: Read raw sources and compile wiki pages
- Query: Answer questions with citations
- Lint: Detect dead links, orphans, staleness
